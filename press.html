RMS - Route Management System : In the News

June 2019 - RMS announces a new website and app.

06/27/2019

After only four working days, the newly-hired team of data scientists and developers have released their MVP of the new corporate website and Route Management System(tm) Web App

June 2019 - RMS announces hiring of data scientists and web developers.

06/21/2019

RMS has assembled a team of the top data scientists and developers available. No expense was spared. They are tasked with the seemingly impossible goal of getting a new corporate website up to market our app. The app will gather data from all 50 states and provide real-time updates on delays due to construction, traffic, accidents, and weather. Details on the new team can be found at: Meet the Team

Management expects it should take no more than four working days to complete.

April 2019 - RMS considers entering the digital age.

RMS is considering a new strategy after another quarter of declining sales of their flagship product, the 800 page monthly "Routes and Routing." A consultant has suggested transitioning their product to a web-based application.

Additional consultants will be hired soon to explain to management what the "web" is and why they should use it.
